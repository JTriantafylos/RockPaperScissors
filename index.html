Rules

You will play Rock, Paper, Scissors against 5 different opponents.

To win the game you must defeat all 5 opponents before the computer reaches a score of 5.

To beat an opponent and move up a level you must reduce their health to 0.

For an opponent to beat you and gain 1 score they must reduce your health to 0.

As you beat opponents they will become better at correctly predicting what you are going to choose.

Opponent 1 will not have a chance at correctly predicting your choice.
Opponent 2 will have a 5% chance.
Opponent 3 will have a 10% chance.
Opponent 4 will have a 15% chance.
Finally, Opponent 5 will have a 25% chance.

When a round is won a random amount of damage, between 1 and 5, will be done to the loser of the round.

Once the game is over or you would like to restart, click the restart button in the top-left of the screen.
